The app Orange Fitness - intended to organize training in the fitness club.

Capabilities:

  • Systematization your workouts.
  • Making your own diets and meal plans.
  • Preparation of individual programs, depending on the goal you are pursuing (muscle set, burning fat, increasing the force or maintenance of relief, etc.).
